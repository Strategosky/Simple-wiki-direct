Simple Wiki Direct

This project exists to unlock the full potential of Wikipedia's "Simple English" articles. Standard Wikipedia links often lead to highly detailed, lengthy pages that can easily distract from your main reading when you only need a brief overview.

Use this extension to display a subtle popup over standard Wikipedia links. With one click, you are directed to the concise, easy-to-read "Simple" version of that topic without breaking your focus. Happy reading.

Download Extension (ZIP)
>_ SYSTEM SETUP / INSTALLATION
  1. Click the download button above and unzip the folder.
  2. Open Firefox and type about:debugging in the URL bar.
  3. Click This Firefox on the left menu.
  4. Click Load Temporary Add-on...
  5. Select the manifest.json file from your unzipped folder.
