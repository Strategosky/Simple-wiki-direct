Simple
Wiki Direct.

This project exists to unlock the full potential of Wikipedia's "Simple English" articles. Standard Wikipedia links often lead to highly detailed, lengthy pages that easily distract from your main reading when you only need a brief overview.

Use this extension to display a subtle popup over standard Wikipedia links. With one click, you are directed to the concise, easy-to-read "Simple" version of that topic without breaking your focus. Happy reading.

Installation Details
01. Extract
Download the archive and extract the directory to your local filesystem.
02. Access
Launch Firefox and navigate to "about:debugging" in the URL bar.
03. Target
Select "This Firefox" from the sidebar, then click "Load Temporary Add-on".
04. Inject
Select the "manifest.json" file to inject the extension into your browser.
